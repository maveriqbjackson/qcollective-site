The maveriQ Doctrine

Four connected pieces.

State · Flagship

Colorado Economic Security Act

The seven pillars of life stability for every Coloradan — health, income, family, housing, food, opportunity, and protection from disruption — through a TABOR-compliant enterprise-fund model, no new taxes, voluntary participation.

US-RAPA

The United States Rapid Action and Preparedness Act — a democracy-protection framework focused on citizen empowerment, election security, and putting real power back in the people’s hands at the national level.

Our Standard

We don't pick sides — we pick balance. Every bill we build or back has to work for everyone it touches: the worker and the business, the farmer and the grocer, the family and the future. Evidence over ideology. People over party. If it only wins by making someone lose, it isn't ready.
