The Q Score

How the score works.

Every score is built from four parts, openly. AI assists by reading each bill and its voting record; the formula stays fixed and public.

40%

Pillar Alignment

How closely the bill and the lawmaker’s position advance the seven pillars of life stability.

25%

Attendance

Whether the lawmaker shows up and votes on the bills that matter.

25%

Citizen Impact

The real, measurable effect of the legislation on everyday people’s lives.

10%

Sponsorship

Whether the lawmaker leads — sponsoring and co-sponsoring legislation that helps.

A score of 80 or higher earns the designation of Accountability Champion — a lawmaker whose record consistently serves the stability of the people they represent.

Our Standard

We don't pick sides — we pick balance. Every bill we build or back has to work for everyone it touches: the worker and the business, the farmer and the grocer, the family and the future. Evidence over ideology. People over party. If it only wins by making someone lose, it isn't ready.
